Name: Vansh Prakash Machhi  |  Roll No: 33

NyayaSetu – A Web-Based Platform for Automated Case Management, Legal Education & AI-Driven Advice

NyayaSetu is your retro-inspired, go-to legal assistant—no jargon, no paperwork headaches. Enter a case number and instantly see all details, from hearing dates to judgements, in your dashboard without wading through PDFs or manual entry.

Document creation is instant: Just enter simple info and NyayaSetu crafts ready-to-file PDFs like affidavits and rent agreements, so you spend less time with repetitive forms and more time on actual casework.

Aspiring lawyers enjoy the Learning Hub for classic law notes, quizzes, and knowledge bites. The AI-powered chat gives you a clear summary and next steps for every legal query—always reminding you this is guidance, not a courtroom substitute.

Tech Stack: Django, Bootstrap, MySQL, OpenAI API, HTML5, CSS3, WeasyPrint.
Hardware: Dual-core CPU, 4GB RAM, 500MB disk, 1024x768+ display.
Software: Python 3.10+, VS Code, pip, Chrome/Firefox/Edge.

Conclusion: NyayaSetu makes law easy, interactive, and accessible. From document generation to legal learning and automated case import, it’s proof that technology can bridge the gap for students, advocates, and the public alike.
